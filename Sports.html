Rock Climbing

Rock Climbing is a sport that has been around for many years, it was in the 1880's that Europe began to develop rock climbing as a sport separate from the traditional mountaineering.
There are many different types of rock climbing, my interests lye mostly in Bouldering and Lead Climbing. I started climbing at the age of 16 in my local climbing wall with my brother and father. My interest for the sport deepened when I joined the UCC rock climbing/mountaineering club.
Rock Climbing is a competitive sport although I have no interest in competing. There is also a standardised grading system in rock climbing, it ranges from 1 to 9a+. Each route has a different difficulty, if you are capable of completing the climb then that is your grade. I am a 5c on the grading scale.
